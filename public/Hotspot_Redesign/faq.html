Application Process

Applying is simple and takes just a few minutes:

  1. Create a free account on Hotspot Finance
  2. Fill in your basic information and financing requirements
  3. Receive instant offers from multiple financial institutions
  4. Compare offers and select the one that suits you best
  5. Complete the process with the chosen bank

Typically, you will need:

  • Valid Saudi National ID or Iqama
  • Salary certificate or proof of income
  • Bank statements for the last 3 months
  • Employment details

Requirements may vary depending on the type of financing and the financial institution.

You can receive initial offers within minutes of submitting your application. Final approval times depend on the financial institution and the type of financing you choose. Personal loans can be approved within hours, while mortgages may take a few business days.

Our initial comparison process uses a soft credit check that does not affect your credit score. A hard credit inquiry is only performed once you choose an offer and proceed with the full application at the partner bank.

APR (Annual Percentage Rate) is the total annual cost of borrowing, expressed as a percentage. It includes the interest rate and any associated fees. A lower APR means less overall cost. Our platform makes it easy to compare APR rates across different offers so you can make an informed decision.

Eligibility & Requirements

Generally, you are eligible if you:

  • Are a Saudi citizen or resident with a valid Iqama
  • Are at least 18 years old
  • Have a stable source of income
  • Meet the minimum salary requirements set by the financial institution

Specific eligibility criteria may vary by product and bank.

Minimum salary requirements vary by financial institution and the type of financing. Typically, the minimum monthly salary starts from SAR 4,000 for personal finance. Use our platform to see which offers match your income level.

Yes, expatriates with a valid Iqama and meeting the required criteria can apply for financing through Hotspot Finance. Available products and terms may differ from those offered to Saudi nationals, depending on the bank's policies.
