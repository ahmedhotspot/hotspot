SME Financing Products

Hotspot offers access to a comprehensive range of SME financing products tailored to different business needs:

  • Working Capital Finance: Short-term financing (typically 12 to 36 months) designed to cover operational expenses such as payroll, inventory purchases, rent, and supplier payments. Ideal for businesses experiencing seasonal demand fluctuations or rapid growth.
  • Equipment & Asset Finance: Medium to long-term financing (up to 7 years) for purchasing business-critical equipment, vehicles, and machinery. The financed equipment often serves as collateral, reducing the need for additional guarantees.
  • POS-Based Finance: A modern financing product that uses your point-of-sale transaction data to determine eligibility and loan amount. Repayments are collected automatically as a fixed percentage of your daily card sales, making it ideal for retail and hospitality businesses.
  • Contract Finance: Financing provided against awarded contracts from government entities or reputable private companies. This product allows you to fund the execution of projects without depleting your cash reserves, with repayment linked to contract payment milestones.
  • Invoice Financing: Convert your outstanding invoices into immediate cash. Instead of waiting 60 to 90 days for clients to pay, you can access up to 90% of the invoice value upfront and receive the remainder (minus fees) when the client pays.

Eligibility Requirements

SME finance eligibility criteria vary by product and institution, but general requirements include:

  • Business Registration: Active Commercial Registration (CR) with the Ministry of Commerce, and valid GOSI registration
  • Business Age: Minimum of 12 months in operation (some products require 6 months for POS-based financing)
  • Annual Revenue: Minimum annual revenue of SAR 100,000 (varies by product; some institutions accept lower for micro-enterprises)
  • Financial Statements: Audited or management-prepared financial statements for the last 1 to 2 years
  • Credit History: Clean SIMAH record for the business and its owners/guarantors
  • Business Classification: Must fall within the SAMA definition of SME (annual revenue up to SAR 200 million and fewer than 250 employees)
  • Sector Restrictions: Some sectors may be excluded depending on the institution's risk appetite

Kafalah Program: Government-Backed Guarantee

The Kafalah Program is a government initiative managed by the SME Bank (formerly Kafalah Program) that provides loan guarantees to SMEs, reducing the risk for lending institutions and making it easier for businesses to access financing. Under Kafalah, the government guarantees up to 80% of the financing amount (up to SAR 1.6 million), which means banks can offer more favorable terms including lower profit rates, reduced collateral requirements, and higher approval rates. When comparing offers on Hotspot, look for the Kafalah badge to identify products that are backed by this program.

Preparing Your Application

To increase your chances of approval and secure the best terms, prepare your application thoroughly. Ensure your financial statements are up to date and accurately reflect your business performance. Organize your bank statements for the last 6 to 12 months showing consistent revenue. If applying for contract financing, have copies of your awarded contracts ready. A well-prepared application demonstrates professionalism and significantly speeds up the approval process.
