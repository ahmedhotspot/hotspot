About the Offer

SAB Bank (formerly HSBC Saudi Arabia) brings international banking expertise to its personal financing program. With the highest financing cap in the market at 3,000,000 SAR and an APR of 3.40%, SAB is ideal for customers with premium financing needs. Backed by a global banking network and a commitment to service excellence, SAB delivers a financing experience tailored to professionals and high-income earners who require substantial funding with premium-level support.

Key Benefits

  • Highest Financing Cap: Access up to 3,000,000 SAR in personal financing, one of the highest limits available among Saudi banks, ideal for large-scale financial needs.
  • Rewards Program: Earn loyalty points on your financing that can be redeemed for travel, shopping, or cashback through SAB's integrated rewards platform.
  • Premium Customer Service: Enjoy dedicated relationship managers and priority service channels designed for a seamless and personalized banking experience.
  • Global Banking Network: Benefit from SAB's affiliation with the HSBC Group, gaining access to international banking services and cross-border financial expertise.
  • Competitive Early Settlement: SAB offers attractive early settlement options with reduced profit margins, allowing you to close your financing ahead of schedule.
  • Sharia-Compliant: All financing products are structured under Murabaha principles and certified by SAB's independent Sharia Supervisory Committee.

Eligibility

  • Saudi nationals or residents with a valid Iqama
  • Minimum age of 21 years
  • Minimum monthly salary of 7,000 SAR
  • Employed for at least 6 months with current employer
  • Good credit standing with SIMAH

Required Documents

  • Valid national ID or Iqama
  • Salary certificate from your employer (issued within the last 30 days)
  • Bank statements for the last 6 months
  • Proof of residence (utility bill or rental contract)
  • Employment contract or HR letter confirming your position and tenure

All rates, amounts, and terms are subject to SAB Bank's credit assessment and final approval. Monthly payments shown are estimated based on a 100,000 SAR financing amount and may vary depending on the approved rate and tenure.
