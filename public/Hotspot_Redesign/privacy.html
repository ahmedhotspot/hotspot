Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Last Updated: April 1, 2026

1. Introduction

Hotspot Digital Marketplace LLC ("Hotspot", "we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our platform, mobile application, and related services. Hotspot is licensed by the Saudi Central Bank (SAMA) and complies with all applicable data protection regulations in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Full name, national ID number, date of birth, email address, phone number, and residential address.
  • Financial Information: Employment details, monthly salary, existing liabilities, and credit history (obtained through authorized credit bureau checks).
  • Device & Usage Data: IP address, browser type, operating system, pages visited, and interaction data for analytics and improvement purposes.
  • Cookies & Tracking: We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience and analyze usage patterns.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To verify your identity and assess your eligibility for financing products.
  • To match you with personalized financing offers from our partner banks and financial institutions.
  • To communicate with you regarding your applications, offers, and account updates.
  • To improve our platform, services, and user experience through analytics.
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements, including SAMA regulations.

4. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We may share your information with:

  • Partner Banks & Financial Institutions: To process your financing applications and provide you with tailored offers.
  • Credit Bureaus: Such as SIMAH (Saudi Credit Bureau) for credit assessment purposes.
  • Technology Partners: Including ELM, NIC, and other authorized service providers for identity verification.
  • Regulatory Authorities: When required by law or regulation, including SAMA.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Data Security

We implement bank-grade security measures to protect your personal data, including:

  • End-to-end encryption for all data transmissions (SSL/TLS).
  • Secure data storage with access controls and regular security audits.
  • Multi-factor authentication for account access.
  • Regular vulnerability assessments and penetration testing.

6. Your Rights

Under Saudi data protection laws, you have the right to:

  • Access and review your personal data held by us.
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal retention requirements).
  • Withdraw consent for data processing at any time.
  • Lodge a complaint with the relevant regulatory authority.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. Financial records are retained as required by SAMA regulations.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
