About the Offer

Al Rajhi Bank offers one of the most competitive personal financing solutions in the Kingdom. With an annual percentage rate starting at just 2.99%, you can access up to 2,500,000 SAR to fund your personal goals, whether it's home renovation, education, travel, or debt consolidation. The financing is fully Sharia-compliant and designed to provide maximum flexibility for salaried individuals across all sectors.

Key Benefits

  • Instant Approval: Get your financing approved within minutes through the digital application process, with funds transferred directly to your account.
  • No Guarantor Required: Apply independently without the need for a co-signer or guarantor, simplifying the entire process.
  • Optional Salary Transfer: You are not required to transfer your salary to Al Rajhi Bank in order to qualify for this financing offer.
  • Early Settlement: Pay off your remaining balance ahead of schedule with competitive early settlement terms and reduced overall profit charges.
  • Takaful Insurance: Your financing comes with built-in Takaful (Islamic insurance) coverage, providing peace of mind throughout your repayment period.
  • Sharia-Compliant: All financing structures are reviewed and approved by Al Rajhi Bank's Sharia Board, ensuring full compliance with Islamic finance principles.

Eligibility

  • Saudi nationals or residents with a valid Iqama
  • Minimum age of 21 years
  • Minimum monthly salary of 4,000 SAR
  • Employed for at least 3 months with current employer
  • Good credit history with SIMAH

Required Documents

  • Valid national ID or Iqama
  • Salary certificate or recent salary slip (within the last 3 months)
  • Bank statements for the last 3 months
  • Employer identification letter

The rates shown are indicative and subject to change based on your individual credit profile, salary level, and employment sector. Final terms will be confirmed upon application review by Al Rajhi Bank.
